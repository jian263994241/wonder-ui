### ActivityIndicator

| 参数	|说明	|类型	|默认值
| --- | --- | --- | ---
| color | 按钮颜色 | 'inherit' \| 'primary' \| 'secondary' \| 'success' \| 'danger' \| 'warning' \| 'info' | inherit
| iconSize | 图标尺寸 | 'small' \| 'medium' \| 'large' |
| text | 文案 | string |
| type | 类型 | 'spinner' \| 'circular' |
| vertical | 垂直 | boolean | false
