### SvgIcon

| 参数	|说明	|类型	|默认值
| --- | --- | --- | ---
| component | root element | `ElementType` | svg
| size | 尺寸 | `'inherit' \| 'large' \| 'medium' \| 'small'` | medium
| spin | 旋转动画 | `boolean` |
| titleAccess | 标题 | `string` |
| viewBox | viewBox | `string` | '0 0 16 16'
